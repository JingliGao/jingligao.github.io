About Me

Jingli Gao is an associate professor at the School of Software Engineering, Pingdingshan University. He received his B.E. and M.Sc. degree from Henan University, and Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University. His research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning (deep learning), computer vision and graphics, robotics, data visualization. He has published more than 10 SCI/EI indexed papers, presided over 1 National Natural Science Foundation of China, 1 Science and Technology Research Project of Henan Province, and 1 key scientific research project of Colleges and Universities of Henan Province.

Research Interests

Include artificial intelligence, machine learning (deep learning), computer vision and graphics, robotics, data visualization, and their applications in intelligent security (e.g., object detection and tracking, activity recognition), intelligent medical care (e.g., medical image understanding and oral digital medical technology), intelligent manufacturing (e.g., product quality testing, product intelligent rating, product production process visualization), and intelligent transportation (e.g., traffic scene anomaly detection, road vehicle violation detection, traffic sign auxiliary detection, driver behavior detection).
