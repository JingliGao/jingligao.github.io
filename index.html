Jingli Gao (高敬礼) [CV], an associate professor with the School of Software Engineering, Pingdingshan University (PDSU), Pingdingshan, China. Dr. Gao is the director of Visual Computing and Machine Learning Group (VCML Group). He received the Ph.D. degree in control theory and control engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in December 2017. His current research interests include Artificial intelligence, machine learning (deep learning), computer vision, multimedia information processing, medical image analysis, robotics. Dr. Cong was a recipient of the Leader of Academic Technology of Henan Provincial Department of Education (河南省教育厅学术技术带头人), Leader of Academic Technology of Pingdingshan City-Pingdingshan City (平顶山市学术技术带头人), Outstanding Young Technologist of Pingdingshan City (平顶山市青年科技专家).
